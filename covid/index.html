what is coronavirus?

Coronavirus is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered covid-19.This new disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan(china).in december 2019.

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses that are known to cause illness in amimals or human.In humans,several coronavirus are know to cause respiratory infectious ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS).

COVID-19 (Precaution)

6 Steps Prevention Against (Corona-Virus)

Covering your mouth and nose with mask when you cough & sneeze
Wash your hands regularly for 20 (Sec) with soap and water or alcohol-based hand wash
Avoid close contact 1-meter or 3-meters with others people who are felling unwell
Stay connected with newspaper and tv (News-channel) to Update yourself and follow required guideline
Stay home and self isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell
If you feel tired and have a fever with cold as well as cough then immediately consult with doctors as soon
